Portsmouth finds itself in the midst of exciting times.

There are lots of small grassroots projects, many enthusiastic people who are working hard and attempting to do big things – important things – while also working in their day jobs, studying and looking after their families.

There is an abundance of passion and enthusiasm to contribute to positive change for the environment and people living here in Portsmouth, but perhaps, for some projects, there is also a lack of focused skills and support to be able to achieve these goals.

Examples of challenges people face:

  • Projects that struggle to move into action and results.
  • Gaps in marketing, project management, technology skills.

We are a small group of people committed to inclusivity and social justice, respecting resource limits and creating resilience in our community.

We believe in using creative ways to engage and involve people, encouraging them to imagine the future they want to inhabit. We want to help make things happen and are looking to gift our time to local projects who are seeking environmental, social and economic solutions to some of the challenges of our times.

We are taking expressions of interest from anyone who thinks they could use our skills to get their project off the ground, past any blocks, or to the next level of action and results. Please contact us for an informal discussion of how we might work together.
